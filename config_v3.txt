Accepted wifi_power values are: -1, 2, 5, 7, 8.5, 11, 13, 15, 17, 18.5, 19, 19.5 dBm.

Accepted avg_measurements values: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 12, 15, 20, 30, 60

sea_level_altitude is in meters and it must be changed according to the current location of the device. 122.0 meters is the average altitude in Milan, Italy.

The timezone follows the standard definition in the tz timezone.
More details at https://www.gnu.org/software/libc/manual/html_node/TZ-Variable.html
